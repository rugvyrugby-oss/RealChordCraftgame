privacy policy

last updated: 22 may 2026

this policy explains what information chordcraft collects and how it's used. by using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. what we collect

email address — if you join a waitlist or sign up, we store your email so we can contact you about the service.

payment information — if you buy pro, payment is handled by gumroad. we never see or store your card details. gumroad processes the payment and gives us confirmation that you purchased.

generations — the prompts you type and chords you generate are processed to create your results. we don't sell or publicly share what you create.

2. how we use it

to run the app, deliver your chord progressions, manage your subscription, respond to support, and occasionally email you about the service. that's it.

3. who we share with

we use a few third-party services to run chordcraft:

— anthropic (the ai that generates the chords)

— gumroad (handles pro payments)

— netlify (hosts the app)

these services process data only to make the app work. we don't sell your data to anyone, ever.

4. cookies and local storage

the app stores some data in your browser (like your settings, daily usage count, and saved projects). this stays on your device and isn't sent to us.

5. your rights

you can ask us to delete your email or any data we hold about you at any time. just email the address below.

6. data security

we take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no online service is 100% secure. use the app at your own discretion.

7. children

chordcraft isn't directed at children under 13. if you're under 13, please don't sign up or make purchases without a parent's involvement.

8. changes

we may update this policy occasionally. continued use after changes means you accept the updated version.

9. governing law

this policy is governed by the laws of australia.
